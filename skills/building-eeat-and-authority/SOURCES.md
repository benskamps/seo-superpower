## [1] AI Overviews and E-E-A-T — the 96% citation gate

- **wellows.com — "Google AI Overviews Ranking Factors: 2026 Guide to Winning Citations"** [study/secondary]
  https://wellows.com/blog/google-ai-overviews-ranking-factors/
  Source of the headline "96% of AI Overview citations come from sources with strong E-E-A-T signals" with r=0.81 correlation. Frames E-E-A-T as a pass/fail filter at Stage 3 of the AI Overview pipeline rather than a gradient.

- **ALM Corp — "Google AI Overview Citations From Top-10 Pages Dropped From 76% to 38%" (2026)** [study]
  https://almcorp.com/blog/google-ai-overview-citations-drop-top-ranking-pages-2026/
  Companion data showing AI Overviews increasingly surface long-tail and structured content classic SEO underrates — strengthening the case that E-E-A-T (not pure rank) is the gate.

## [2] Person schema, sameAs, and AI citation lift

- **automateed.com — "How to Add Schema Markup for Authors: Complete Guide 2026"** [study/secondary]
  https://www.automateed.com/how-to-add-schema-markup-for-authors
  Source of the "verified `sameAs` link → 2.8× citation likelihood" finding and the "Article with declared author cited at 94% confidence vs 61% for plain text" claim. Also sources the 40-site / 38% median citation lift study.

- **redot.global — "Build E-E-A-T Authority: 2026 Strategy for Google & AI"** [secondary]
  https://redot.global/blog/eeat-authority-google-ai-trust-signals/

- **upGrowth — "Schema Markup for GEO: 9 Citation Patterns [2026]"** [secondary]
  https://upgrowth.in/schema-markup-geo-9-patterns-ai-citations-2026/

## [3] Google's Experience addition and quality rater framework

- **Google Search Central — "Our latest update to the quality rater guidelines: E-A-T gets an extra E for Experience" (Dec 2022)** [primary]
  https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2022/12/google-raters-guidelines-e-e-a-t
  The original announcement adding "Experience" to the framework. Anchors the claim that Experience is weighted heavily for product reviews, travel, finance, and personal-narrative content.

- **Google — "Search Quality Rater Guidelines: An Overview" PDF** [primary]
  https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/hsw-sqrg.pdf
  The current public quality rater overview. Authoritative source for the four-pillar framework and the "trust is the most important member" stance.

- **broworks.net — "Google's 2026 Search Quality Rater Guidelines: What You Need to Know"** [secondary]
  https://www.broworks.net/blog/googles-2026-search-quality-rater-guidelines-what-you-need-to-know
  Synthesis of the most recent QRG update (Sept 2025), including the formal expansion of YMYL to cover government information and elections.
